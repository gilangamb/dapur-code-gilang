# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People who want premium, instant nail care ("tampil slay setiap saat"). They value aesthetics, convenience, and high-quality handcrafted products.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Kukururu's handmade press-on nails and nail art services, build brand trust, and drive bookings/purchases through WhatsApp or Shopee.

## Brand Personality
Modern, playful, chic, handcrafted, and clean.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS landing pages, corporate or overly busy e-commerce templates. Monotone or purely clinical aesthetics without personality.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell: Let the high-quality imagery of the nails speak for the brand.
- Practice what you preach: The website itself should feel as crafted and premium as the products.
- Playful but clean: Use doodles and accents thoughtfully without overwhelming the core content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA compliance. Ensure the body text has sufficient contrast against the cream paper background. Ensure clear focus states for interactive elements.
